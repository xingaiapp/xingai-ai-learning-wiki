## Scoring

| Dimension | Weight | Evidence |
|---|---:|---|
| Explain | 15 | Accurate 5W + How explanation at the target depth |
| Implement | 20 | Working code, types, tests, and reproducible setup |
| Evaluate | 15 | Baseline, representative cases, metrics, and error analysis |
| Debug | 15 | Hypothesis-driven isolation and verified correction |
| Design | 15 | Clear diagram, boundaries, alternatives, and tradeoffs |
| Secure and operate | 10 | Threats, controls, telemetry, runbook, and residual risk |
| Lead and communicate | 10 | Outcome ownership, decisions, disagreement, and audience fit |

Passing score is 80/100 with no dimension below 60%. Courses 06–09 additionally require zero unresolved critical safety or authorization defects.

## Five Performance Levels

1. **Novice:** names concepts but cannot apply them reliably.
2. **Developing:** completes guided work but misses edge cases.
3. **Independent:** delivers and explains a bounded production-quality solution.
4. **Senior:** anticipates failures, quantifies tradeoffs, and improves team decisions.
5. **Strategic:** shapes portfolios, operating models, and risk decisions across the organization.

## Assessment Types

- Diagnostic: ungraded baseline before each course.
- Knowledge check: short explanations and calculations.
- Code review: correctness, tests, safety, and maintainability.
- Lab: reproducible implementation and evidence.
- Design defense: 30-minute presentation plus challenge questions.
- Interview simulation: timed rubric-calibrated loop.
- Reflection: what changed, what remains uncertain, and the next experiment.

## Integrity

AI assistance is allowed when disclosed. Learners must identify generated sections, verify claims and code, explain every submitted decision, and complete a live modification without assistance. Unsupported generated work receives no credit.
