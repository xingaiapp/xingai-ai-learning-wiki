# Course 08: AI Leadership And CTO Strategy

**Prerequisite:** [07](07-enterprise-decision-systems.md) · **Gate:** board-level strategy defense · **Next:** [09](09-ai-interview-mastery.md)

Portfolio-level judgment: "invest when a valuable decision can improve, evidence and ownership exist, and the organization can operate the system. Pause when the use case lacks a measurable outcome or accountable owner." The `opportunity_score()` code (weighted value/feasibility/readiness/risk, explicitly *not* a replacement for accountable judgment) teaches: score, but don't let the score make the call alone.

The failure list — "strategy by demo count," "governance detached from delivery," "ROI without baselines" — is a rebuke of treating a ranking table as a decision by itself.
